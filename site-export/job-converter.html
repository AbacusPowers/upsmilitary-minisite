Suggested UPS Jobs

Package Handler

The package handler loads and unloads packages into or out of UPS vehicles. In this physical and fast-paced position, package handlers continually lift, lower and slide packages weighing between 25 and 70 pounds.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Driver Helper (October–December)

The driver helper performs the task of delivering and picking up UPS packages. This is a seasonal, part-time position with a wealth of opportunities.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Package Delivery Driver

Package delivery drivers must have excellent customer contact and driving skills, including the ability to operate a vehicle equipped with a standard (manual) transmission. As a UPS driver, you’re an ambassador to the community, to neighbors and to friends.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Feeder Driver

Feeder drivers play a critical role in UPS operations by transporting customers’ packages between our centers and hubs. This position involves driving a tractor-trailer unit to one or more destinations, and then returning to the original hub during the same work shift.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Automotive Mechanic

UPS mechanics ensure we’re able to meet and exceed all challenges, efficiently and effectively. UPS automotive mechanics must be at least 21 years old, be proficient at operating standard transmission vehicles, meet Department of Transportation driving requirements and meet other conditions as required by job assignment.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Facilities Mechanic

Facilities maintenance mechanics at UPS help maintain our infrastructure and facilities to ensure we’re able to meet and exceed all challenges. This position may require experience working with three-phase motors; electrical troubleshooting; conveyors; and arc, MIG and acetylene welding and cutting.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Sales Representative

As a UPS sales representative, you can feel confident selling for a company that sets the standard in reliability. Employees in this position use available resources to learn customers’ businesses, identify business needs and position UPS’ small package products and services to meet their needs.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Warehouse Associate

UPS warehouse associates participate in a wide range of daily operations, including loading and unloading, inbound processing, scanning orders, and inventory control. This position may require you to operate powered equipment such as a forklift or hydraulic jack lift to perform duties.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

UPS Freight Over-the-Road (OTR) Driver

UPS OTR drivers operate tractor-trailers for the over-the-road delivery of freight to two or more locations. These drivers are typically on the road seven to 10 days at a time and normally pull 53-foot van trailers. OTR drivers must pass a Department of Transportation physical and UPS Freight road test, plus meet other conditions as required by job assignment.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Part-Time Operations Supervisor

The part-time operations supervisor provides innovative and effective leadership while directly supervising seven to 10 part-time package handlers. Employees in this part-time position train, develop and hold their work groups responsible for safety, production and attendance.

Search to see openings for this job near you:

Business Analyst

At UPS, our business systems analysts serve as liaisons between the business community and UPS Information Services. This position contributes to the identification of solutions, eliciting and developing functional requirements to meet the needs of the business in a date-driven business environment.
