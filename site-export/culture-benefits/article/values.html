The shared values of UPS and the military

Among the many reasons U.S. veterans thrive here is a mutual belief in doing things right.

Integrity

In the military, it's all about honor. At UPS, it's who we are and the foundation of all we do.

Teamwork

In the military, completing missions as a team is a way of life. At UPS, determined people working together can accomplish anything.

Service

In the military, putting others before self is the very definition of service. At UPS, meeting the needs of our customers and communities is central to our success.

Accountability

In the military, you have a duty to your teammates, and no one leaves anyone behind. At UPS, we take responsibility for our actions, for each other's safety, and we all grow together.

Quality & Efficiency

In the military, precision leads to excellence, and cutting corners is never an option. At UPS, we never stop pursuing faster, smarter and better solutions.

Diversity & Opportunity

In the military, diverse backgrounds and beliefs meld into one united force. At UPS, our strength starts with tolerance and respect for all and anyone who works hard can rise through our ranks.
